My 7 Roles at Praskla Technology

As Director of Cybersecurity, I lead all security strategy, standards, and enforcement. As Secure Full-Stack Developer, I build products end-to-end with security embedded from day one.

As Project Lead, I own delivery and team alignment for SELMA. As QA Lead, I ensure that every release meets stringent quality expectations. As DevSecOps Engineer, I secure every pipeline and CI/CD process.

As Security Trainer & Mentor, I grow the next generation of secure developers at Praskla. As Business & Product Strategist, I drive product vision and align client requirements with technical execution.
